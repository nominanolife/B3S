Ready to take your Online TDC?

Online TDC is a platform where you can learn and practice the Traffic Discipline Course (TDC) online. The course is designed to help you understand the rules and regulations of the road and to help you become a better driver. The course is divided into several modules, each covering a different aspect of driving. You can take the course at your own pace and complete it in your own time. Once you have completed the course, you will be awarded a certificate of completion which you can use to show that you have successfully completed the course.

Ready to take your Online TDC exam?

Here are some important details about the exam:

  • Must complete the full Theoretical Driving Course.
  • 120 multiple-choice questions.
  • Minimum 80% (96 correct answers out of 120).
  • Unlimited retakes until passing.
  • After 3 consecutive failures, review course materials before retaking.
  • Immediate result display; performance evaluation generated by AI.
