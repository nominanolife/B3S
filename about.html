About Us

B3S Driving School is an LTO accredited Driving School that provides effective driving courses to aspiring drivers. We make sure to equip our students with the right knowledge and proper skills that every Responsible Driver should have. Beginning with our Theoretical Driving Course that can be completed online or Face to Face as well as the Practical Driving Courses, we help the driver get acquainted with the basics of driving a motor vehicle and develop driving skills with better discipline and mastery.

We are able to provide a better and suitable environment along with instructional materials, road safety education, and with the help and guidance of our professional LTO accredited Driving Instructors.

Our courses will allow you to be more comfortable at driving and be a confidently responsible Driver.

Enroll now at B3S Driving School and together let us make a safer Driver on the road. Not just for ourselves but for every individual on the road.
