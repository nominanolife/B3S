Courses

THEORETICAL DRIVING COURSE

A theoretical driving course provides foundational knowledge about road safety, traffic laws, and vehicle operation, preparing students for practical driving and written exams.

PRACTICAL DRIVING COURSE

A practical driving course focuses on hands-on instruction and real-world driving experience, teaching students the skills needed to operate a vehicle safely and confidently on the road.
